Regular Expressions and Languages

Type a regular expression in one of the boxes (they all do the same thing) and hit return to see (part of) the language it describes.

Regular expressionLanguage

Some things to try:

  • a(a|b)*
  • (a|b)*a(a|b)*
  • ((a|b)(a|b))*
  • (a|b)((a|b)(a|b))*
  • b*ab*
  • b*ab*ab*
